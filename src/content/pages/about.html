About Us

Philippine Business Society of Alberta

At the Philippine Business Society of Alberta, we are committed to supporting Filipino entrepreneurs by creating a strong and collaborative business community in Edmonton and across Alberta. Our goal is to connect business owners, foster growth, and provide the tools and resources needed to thrive in the competitive business landscape.

We offer a platform for Filipino entrepreneurs to network, learn from each other, and access valuable business resources. Through events, workshops, and community outreach, we strive to help our members grow their businesses and contribute to the broader economy of Alberta.

By working together, we build a stronger business presence for Filipino entrepreneurs, driving success and innovation in the region.
